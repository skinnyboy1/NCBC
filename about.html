our history

Maud Petit Frere & Ernst Petit Frere

During the years of 2004-2007, Pastor Ernst Petit-Frere and his wife Maud opened their home to individuals in the Haitian community for prayer, bible study and fellowship every Friday, Saturday and Sunday evening. These services began to increase in number. All of the members shared the same desires of creating a name for this group and purchasing a building to worship in.
In 2 Corinthians 5:17 the bible says "Therefore if anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation; old things have passed away; behold all things have become new."
In 2007, the members that gathered in the Pastor's home believed that the words in this verse best described their small community of believers. From this verse they voted on the new name, New Creature Baptist Church. Presently, New Creature Baptist is located on 1313 Sellers Street Philadelphia, Pa 19124. We gather every Sunday morning for Sunday school at 10 a.m. and Sunday morning service from 11a.m.-1p.m.
